SECRET RARES — PROGRESS TRACKER
=================================
Last updated: 2026-05-27 (nz complete)

PURPOSE
-------
Track progress adding secret rares (IR, SIR, SAR, HR, AR) to existing JP sets.
These cards are numbered beyond the set's printedTotal and were skipped during
original set data entry. Each has a JP image, usually has an EN equivalent in
the corresponding EN set's secret rare section.

WORKFLOW PER SET
----------------
1. Download JP secret rare images from Serebii (cards beyond printedTotal)
2. Download EN secret rare images from Serebii (from the EN partner set)
3. Read JP images → fill JP card fields (nameJa, hp, types, rarity, artist, etc.)
4. Match each JP secret rare to its EN equivalent
5. Append card objects to data/cards/{setId}.json
6. Append index entries to data/index.json
7. Mark set DONE below

SEREBII URL FORMAT
------------------
JP image: https://serebii.net/card/{jpSlug}/{n}.jpg
EN image: https://serebii.net/card/{enSlug}/{n}.jpg
Set page: https://serebii.net/card/{slug}/

RARITY MAPPING (JP rarityJa → full English rarity field)
----------------------------------------------------------
IR  → Illustration Rare
SIR → Special Illustration Rare
SAR → Special Art Rare
HR  → Hyper Rare
AR  → Art Rare
RR  → Double Rare
ACE → ACE SPEC
