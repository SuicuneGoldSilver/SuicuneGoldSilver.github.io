EN CARDS FORMAT REFERENCE
--------------------------
- setIdEn: short code (dri, svi, pal, ssp, scr, twm, pre, svp)
- numberEn: "NNN/TTT" format using printed total denominator
- imageEn: "https://images.pokemontcg.io/{apiId}/{n}_hires.png"
  where apiId: sv1=svi, sv2=pal, sv6=twm, sv7=scr, sv8=ssp, sv9=pre, sv10=dri, svp=svp

TRAINER CARD NOTE
-----------------
Recent SV sets (sv4+) only list NEW unique trainer cards in their set; they don't reprint
standard trainers. The base SVI (sv1) set introduced most common trainers (Ultra Ball,
Nest Ball, Switch, PokéGear 3.0, Nemona, Prof's Research, Rare Candy).
PAL (sv2) introduced Iono, Boss's Orders, Super Rod.
TEF (sv5) introduced Buddy-Buddy Poffin (#144, but PRE #101 also used).
SCR (sv7) and SSP (sv8) only have unique character/set-specific trainers.
